Scope — What the shell price includes

The shell price covers the structure, cover, and hardware to assemble on-site. No interior, no insulation, no door. Here is exactly what you get:

Steel geodesic frame
  • Q235 steel, white powder-coated
  • Rated 20+ year service life
  • Pipe scales with size: 5m φ20×1.5mm, 6m/7m φ25×1.5mm, 8m φ32×2mm
Heavier pipe upgrades available as an add-on if you need extra load capacity.
Two-layer PVC outer cover
  • 70% opaque PVC (850 g/m²) — white, dark green, or beige — waterproof & UV-resistant
  • 30% clear PVC (950 g/m²) — panoramic bay window effect
Outer cover only — interior insulation needed for year-round use (separate add-on).
Five international fire certifications
  • CAN/ULC-S109-14 (Canada) — included at base price

  • BS7837 (UK) · M1 (France)
Cover fabric only. Structural CSA certification is a separate process, currently TBD.

  • Rectangular metal frame 0.9 m × 2.1 m, built into dome structure

Anchoring hardware & insect strips
  • All bolts & screws to secure frame to deck, slab, or similar

Compliance

Canadian compliance status — Gore Bay, Ontario

Based on OBC 2024 (O.Reg. 163/24), Ontario Fire Code, and Town of Gore Bay building department requirements. Status as of July 2026 quotation.

Confirmed on quote

Fire rating — Cover material

CAN/ULC-S109-14, NFPA701, DIN4102 B1, BS7837, M1 — included in base price. Canadian standard satisfies OBC Article 3.14.1.6. Note: Ontario Fire Code Section 2.9.3.3 separately requires a site-wide fire alarm system or designated fire watch personnel for commercial sleeping accommodation — a fire alarm system is the practical choice for a multi-unit resort.

Review required

Snow load — Standard: 0.5 kN/m²  ·  φ32×2mm upgrade: 1.0 kN/m² (102 kg/m² / 20.9 psf)

Standard frame rated at 51 kg/m² (0.5 kN/m² / 10.4 psf). The φ32×2mm pipe upgrade doubles this to 1.0 kN/m² — 102 kg/m² / 20.9 psf. However, Gore Bay (Manitoulin Island) ground snow load (Ss) is estimated at 2.0–2.5 kN/m² per OBC Supplementary Standard SB-1 — well above even the upgraded rating. P.Eng-stamped mitigation is required before winter occupancy approval. Confirm exact local Ss with the Gore Bay CBO or a licensed Ontario engineer.

Upgrade: 200 km/h confirmed

Wind load — Standard: 120 km/h (74.5 mph)  ·  φ32×2mm upgrade: 200 km/h (124 mph)

Standard frame rated to 120 km/h. With the φ32×2mm pipe upgrade, the rated wind resistance increases to 200 km/h (124 mph), applying to all four sizes. Subject to proper platform/foundation anchoring per manufacturer specifications.

Action required

P.Eng stamp or CSA A277 factory certification

Town of Gore Bay building department requires manufacturer-stamped structural drawings with every permit submission (OBC Division B, Part 4). Two paths: (1) CSA A277 factory certification for the dome model — preferred for multi-unit deployment as it substitutes for individual site P.Eng drawings; (2) Ontario P.Eng review of structural design, foundation, and anchoring for each dome model. Contact CBO Chris Meloche before ordering to confirm which path the town will accept.

Permit required

Building permit — Group C Tourist Accommodation, OBC Part 9

A building permit is mandatory in Gore Bay. Glamping domes used as fixed sleeping accommodation will likely be classified as Group C (Tourist Accommodation) buildings under OBC Part 9, Section 9.36.3.1 — not exempt tent structures. Jurisdiction: Town of Gore Bay (not Manitoulin Planning Board). CBO: Chris Meloche — CBOwestmanitoulin@outlook.com · 705-956-0472. Pre-application meeting strongly recommended before ordering.

Not included

Plumbing & electrical

The modular bathroom pod includes internal piping/wiring inside walls, but site hookup, electrical panel, and plumbing rough-in must be completed by licensed trades to OBC/ESA standards. Each dome electrical connection requires an ESA inspection.

Sourcing manager's flag: The φ32×2mm frame upgrade improves the snow rating to 1.0 kN/m² (102 kg/m² / 20.9 psf) and wind rating to 200 km/h — but the snow rating is still well below Gore Bay’s estimated ground Ss of 2.0–2.5 kN/m². A P.Eng-stamped site-specific calculation is required before winter occupancy. Start with a pre-application meeting: CBO Chris Meloche, CBOwestmanitoulin@outlook.com, 705-956-0472.
